A way of life

In this hectic world we live in with all of its troubles,
which our devices reminds us of frequently on a daily basis,
it might be a good idea to look towards the most chillest of mammals,
a mammal so chill and slow moving, algae grows on it.
Ever heard of a Sloth with burnout? Didn't think so.

The Holy Trinity

The three most powerful species of sloths in order of most to least chill are as follows.

  1. The Pygmy Sloths
  2. Maned Sloths
  3. Pale-throated Sloths

What makes the difference

It could be argued that all the species of sloths have an equal amount of chill
but through extensive research and interviews it has been confirmed that
there's a direct correlation between a higher level of stress hormones and species of sloths
who appear in the lists of critically endangered animals.
Nr.3 on our list is considered almost extinct, therefore pretty stressed and drained of chill.

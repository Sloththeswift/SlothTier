Sloth Facts

  • It takes one month for a sloth to digest one leaf, which are their only source of sustenance
  • Up to 950 bugs can reside within the fur of a sloth
  • It's rationalized that the algae growing on their furs is camouflage
  • A baby sloth stays with its mother for over a year to learn about leaves
  • They generally live up to 30 years
  • The top speed of a Sloth is 2.4km/h
